I am a 1st year PhD student at MIRLAB, part of the School of Computing at Yonsei University, where I work on Generative Models. I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Youngjae Yu.

My recent interest is in building reliable and interpretable foundational models such as LLMs and MLLMs. I am specifically interested in

  • Evaluating and Mitigating Hallucinations of Language Models: Is hallcuination inevitable for LLMs? How can we develop trustful models and correctly evaluate them?
  • Interpreting and Monitoring Model Reasoning: Are language models producing faithful reasoning? If so, how can we leverage this to better understand and improve them? If not, how should we build more trustworthy models?

I am also interested in leveraging models to evaluate or improve other models, and utilizing them to augment human capabilities.
